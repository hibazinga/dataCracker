Recommending...

For active user u (userID – itemID tuple)

  1. Map u to group m
  2. Get the rating value from group m’s rating matrix
  3. Sort items by their ratings
  4. recommend 3 highest rated items to u

Recommending...

For inactive user u (userID – itemID tuple)

  1. Get all the active users AUs that affect u
  2. For each AU, do the steps on the previous page to get her rating RAU for this item i 
  3. Rating( u, i ) = weighted sum WS of RAUs for i

Recommending...

4.  Sort items by their ratings
